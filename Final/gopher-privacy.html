2Information Collection and Use

We collect several different types of information for various purposes to provide and improve our Service to you.

Personal Data

While using our Service, we may ask you to provide us with certain personally identifiable information that can be used to contact or identify you ("Personal Data"). This may include, but is not limited to:

  • Contact Information, including name, address, email address, phone number, and other contact information provided.
  • Account and Profile Information, including name and credentials, privacy settings and notification preferences, pictures, and other information you add to your Gopher profile.
  • Identification Documentation and Signature for Age-Restricted Products, including driver's license or other government-issued identification, which we may process in limited circumstances such as an order for an age-restricted product (e.g. alcohol) and for legal and regulatory compliance.
  • In-App Communications, including the content of in-app messages between customer and worker, SMS/text messages, chats, phone calls, and emails with us related to our Services, customer support inquiries, the date and time of communications, the channel used, call recordings (if applicable), and outcomes or resolutions from those communications.
  • Shared Content, including reviews and ratings of a customer or worker from a completed request, or photos you upload.
  • Payment Information, including credit or debit card information used to make purchases. Payment information is primarily stored and processed by our third-party provider (Stripe); however, we may store limited payment-related information such as the last four digits of a payment card.
  • Activity and Transactions on the platform, including items purchased, services ordered, request order history, special instructions (e.g. delivery preferences), transaction amounts, and promotional codes used or received.
  • Geolocation Information (Gopher Go App Only), if you have consented by enabling location access, including precise location while our apps run in the foreground or background. You may use our Services without enabling precise location; however, this may impact your experience, including delivery accuracy and the availability of certain features. If you have not allowed precise location, we can only determine your approximate location.
  • Device Information, including information about the computers, tablets, phones, and other devices you use to interact with us, internet-protocol address, device-based identifiers, operating system and version, preferred language, hardware model identifiers, and browser type and settings.

We may use your Personal Data to contact you with newsletters, marketing or promotional materials, and other information that may be of interest to you. You may opt out of any or all of these communications by following the unsubscribe link or instructions in any email and/or SMS we send, or by contacting us.

Usage Data

We may also collect information that your browser sends whenever you visit our Service or access it through a mobile device ("Usage Data"). This may include your computer's IP address, browser type and version, the pages of our Service you visit, the time and date of your visit, time spent on those pages, unique device identifiers, and other diagnostic data.

When you access the Service with a mobile device, this Usage Data may include the type of mobile device you use, your mobile device unique ID, the IP address of your mobile device, your mobile operating system, the type of mobile internet browser you use, and other diagnostic data.

Tracking & Cookies Data

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to track activity on our Service and hold certain information. Cookies are files with a small amount of data which may include an anonymous unique identifier, sent to your browser from a website and stored on your device. We also use beacons, tags, and scripts to collect and track information and to improve and analyze our Service.

You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent. However, if you do not accept cookies, you may not be able to use some portions of our Service. Examples of cookies we use:

  • Session Cookies. We use Session Cookies to operate our Service.
  • Preference Cookies. We use Preference Cookies to remember your preferences and various settings.
  • Security Cookies. We use Security Cookies for security purposes.
