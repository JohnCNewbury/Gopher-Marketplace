Gopher Request, made simple.

This is your step-by-step guide to getting anything done — a delivery, a move, a haul, a ride, a hand around the house. You describe what you need, you decide what to pay, and a trusted local Gopher comes to you.

⏱️ Live 24 / 7 / 365 💵 You set the price — 100% goes to your Gopher 🛡️ No charge if no one accepts
🧭
How to use this pageEvery request follows the same 7 steps. Some steps change slightly depending on the service you pick — that's noted inline. Use the menu on the left to jump to any section at any time.
Getting started

Sign in or create your account

Gopher is passwordless — there's nothing to remember. You sign in with a one-time code sent to your phone or email.

  1. Open the app and choose Sign in with phone or Sign in with email.
  2. Enter your number or email and tap Send Code.
  3. Type the 6-digit code we text or email you. That's it — you're in.

New here?

Tap Create an account and add your name, phone, email, address, and date of birth. We'll verify your phone and email with the same quick code step. You'll also tell us how you discovered Gopher — and if a friend referred you, you can enter their 6-digit Gopher User ID (or tap “skip” if you don't have it).

✓
Use your legal nameYour name and date of birth can't be changed after signup, so double-check before saving. If something needs to change later, email support@gophergo.io.
Getting started

Complete your profile

From your dashboard, open Account → Personal Info to review everything on file: your name, email, phone, address, date of birth, and how you found Gopher.

Payment method

You won't need this until your first request. When you're ready, your card is added securely through Stripe, our trusted payment partner — you can pick it (and apply a promo code) right on the Review step.

Step 2 of 7 Job details

Describe the job clearly

This is the most important screen. Your description is exactly what workers read when deciding whether to take your job — so be specific.

✅ A great description

“I need 12 yards of mulch laid in my backyard. Worker must bring their own equipment.” (plus a photo)

🚫 Too vague

“Need mulch spread in my backyard.”

You can attach up to 3 photos so workers know exactly what they're walking into.

Details that change by service

Depending on the category you picked in Step 1, you'll also see one of these:

  • Delivery → Delivery Type. Flip on Age-Restricted if your order contains items like alcohol or tobacco (this turns on ID safeguarding). Flip on Items Purchased if your Gopher needs to buy something for you, then enter the Cost of Goods so they can pay on your behalf.
  • Ride Sharing → Rider Info. Set the number of riders and bags, plus any special instructions for your driver.
  • Moving & Junk Removal → Item Info. Tell us the item count and flag anything hazardous. Hit 5+ items and we'll automatically switch you to multi-item mode.
!
Keep it in the appDon't put your phone number in the description. You can message workers safely through Gopher before and during your request.

Step 4 of 7 Locations

Where it happens

Enter your pickup and destination as needed for your service. When you type an address, Gopher pings the area and shows how many workers are nearby.

  • For Moving, note any stairs at each location so your crew arrives prepared.
  • For flexible deliveries, you can choose “No specific pickup” so your Gopher buys from the closest store.
  • Got more than one stop? Enter the final destination here and list the other stops in your description — we recommend full store addresses, not just names.
📍
Can't enter an address? That's usually a location-permission setting on your computer or phone — allow location access for the Gopher app and Google Maps will recognize the available addresses as you type.
Step 5 of 7 Worker pay

Decide what to pay

Gopher is unlike any other platform: you set the price, and 100% of it goes to your Gopher. Think of your offer as a fair wage, not a tip — your Gopher works for you, not for Gopher, Inc.

How would you like to pay?
Set your own price — you name the flat rate up front. Great for errands, rides & deliveries.
Request bids from local workers — not sure what's fair? Let Gophers send offers and pick the best one.

Bids aren't offered for Delivery or Ride Sharing, or when a job needs more than one worker.

Not sure what to offer? Ask Gopher iQ

On Delivery and Ride Sharing, tap the Gopher iQ suggestion to get a fair-rate estimate built from thousands of similar requests in your area. You always have the final say on the amount.

💡
Why offers matterA typical delivery takes 30–45 minutes, so an offer works out roughly like an hourly wage. If a request isn't getting picked up, nudging the offer up brings in workers from a little further away.

Messaging your Gopher

All messaging happens inside the app, in your Inbox. Before you hire, a worker can ask a clarifying question; once hired, you and your Gopher can chat freely about the job.

🔐
Protect your privacySharing phone numbers, emails, or other personal contact info through in-app messaging is against our terms. If a message asks for something it shouldn't, flag it and contact support. You're never obligated to reply.
After you submit

Confirm & rate

When your Gopher marks the job complete, you'll get an app notification and a text. Tap through to the confirmation screen.

  1. Tap Confirm once you've verified the job was done right.
  2. Rate your Gopher on accuracy, communication, quality, and timeliness — this shapes the jobs they see next, so a job well done deserves 5 stars.
  3. Optionally add them as MY Gopher for next time.
⏳
Confirm promptlyConfirming releases your Gopher's payout, so please do it as soon as the job's done — especially if they paid for items out of pocket. If you don't confirm within 48 hours, a non-refundable auto-confirmation kicks in. Only confirm if the task was truly completed.

Something wrong? Use Dispute instead, add a comment, and give your Gopher a chance to make it right before escalating.

A general-errand delivery where you want to pay your Gopher $30 total (including $20 you're reimbursing for goods):

Good to know

Age-restricted orders & TrustShield

For age-restricted items (like alcohol or tobacco), the law and our policy are strict — and we follow them to the letter.

  • You must agree to the age-restricted waiver at submission.
  • You must be present with a valid government-issued ID at delivery. Contactless delivery is never allowed for these items.
  • If you can't show ID after your Gopher has already purchased the items, there's no refund for the goods or the offer — so don't request these unless your ID will be on hand.

Gopher TrustShield™ — free, and saves you money

TrustShield™

Add the free TrustShield badge to verify your identity once — it confirms you're who you say you are. After that you get:

  • $1 off every age-restricted and ride sharing order.
  • Skip re-entering your ID each time — verify once, use everywhere.

You'll still show your physical ID if it's an age-restricted delivery, but ordering gets much faster.

🛡️
Set up with your legal name to use TrustShield. If you signed up under an alias, email a clear photo of your ID to support@gophergo.io to get verified. TrustShield is an identity-verification mark — not a background or credit check.
Good to know

Safety & the no-charge promise

  • Your money is protected. When a request is accepted, funds move to a secure holding account via Stripe. If the job isn't completed, the authorization is released back to your card.
  • No worker, no charge. If we can't connect you with a Gopher, your request simply expires — with no charge and no authorization.
  • Gopher isn't auto-fulfillment. It's an open marketplace; a worker decides whether your offer is worth it. A fair offer in an active area is usually accepted fast.
  • Cancelling. Once a Gopher marks a job in-progress, cancelling may incur up to a $5 fee; if they've already purchased items, you may owe for those too. See the Terms for full details.
🚩
Never pay outside the appIf a Gopher ever asks for money outside Gopher, that's a terms violation — don't do it, and report it to us right away.
