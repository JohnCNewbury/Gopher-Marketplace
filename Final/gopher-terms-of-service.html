15Counter-Offers & Cost Adjustments

Counter-Offers

On the Gopher Go App, some requests allow counter-offers, capped at 150% of the original offer (with a minimum of $20). The feature is meant to turn an unrealistic offer into a fair one. Abusing it may lead to deactivation. If your counter-offer is not accepted, do not accept the request and then use Cost Adjustment to ask for more — this is a violation.

Standard Gophers are allowed 5 counter-offers per month. Elite, Elite+, and Pro Gophers receive unlimited counter-offers as a Pro perk of their verification tier. The 150% cap described above does not apply to Elite, Elite+, or Pro Gophers, whose counter-offers are not capped.

The counter-offer feature is not to be taken lightly or misused. Submitting a counter-offer is a binding offer to the Requestor. If the Requestor accepts it, their payment method is authorized for the countered amount and a binding agreement is formed between you and the Requestor on those terms. You are then expected to complete the request according to its description, and you will be paid the countered amount upon completion. Submitting counter-offers you do not intend to honor, or failing to perform after a counter-offer is accepted, is a misuse of the feature and may result in cancellation, withheld payment, and deactivation.

Cost Adjustments

If the listed cost of items is not enough to cover the actual cost, the Gopher can make a cost adjustment, which requires Requestor approval. The cost-of-items section is always meant to be a wash — neither to make nor lose money. Your earnings are the "Gopher Offering." Gophers should not purchase items significantly above the entered amount before submitting a cost adjustment, as the adjustment may fail. Gopher, Inc. is not responsible for recovering costs above the authorized amount.
