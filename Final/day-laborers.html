Three things a temp agency won’t do

Gopher isn’t a temp agency taking a cut of every day rate. It’s a local marketplace built on three simple rules.

🤝

Hassle free & haggle free

Make an offer up front or ask for instant bids. Either way you’ll experience the easiest way to find great workers ever — one steady worker for the morning or a crew for the whole day.

💵

Paid directly

Your workers are paid directly and keep 100% of your offer — 0% commission. No agency cut skimmed from their pay, and no minimum head-count to book.

🛠️

Booked to done

A crew you pick by name and rating shows up ready to put in a full day — hauling, clearing, loading, digging — and stays till the job’s done.
